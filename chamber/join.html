NP Membership Benefits

NP Membership is ideal for registered non-profit organizations seeking chamber support without a fee.

  • Access to non-profit networking events
  • Advocacy and community outreach resources
  • Inclusion in chamber digital directories
  • Member-only newsletters and volunteer initiatives

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze Membership offers affordable participation with visibility and chamber support.

  • Basic business listing in the directory
  • Invitations to chamber events
  • Member updates and communications
  • Access to entry-level business resources

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver Membership adds promotional and training advantages for growing organizations.

  • Featured advertising slots on the home page
  • Discounts on workshops and seminars
  • Priority invites to networking events
  • Access to business mentorship programs

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold Membership delivers the highest exposure, premier events, and sponsorship opportunities.

  • Spotlight advertising on the homepage
  • Exclusive leadership events and dinners
  • Early access to sponsorships and partnerships
  • Complimentary training and executive briefings
